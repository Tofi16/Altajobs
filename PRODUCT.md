# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Workers and job seekers looking for a trusted place to create a professional profile, secure verification, and manage premium wallet-based services. The interface should feel aimed at people who want confidence in the work opportunities they discover and the credibility they present.

## Product Purpose

AltaJobs exists to connect users with verified work opportunities while helping them build trust through profile verification, premium CV services, and a secure in-app wallet. Success looks like users quickly understanding how to upgrade their credibility and engaging with the verification flow.

## Positioning

A modern, trustworthy career platform that combines verified worker profiles, premium payments, and social trust signals in a single experience.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: Create your worker profile
- Secondary CTA: Explore verified opportunities or upgrade with wallet-backed verification
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: AltaJobs is the place to build a verified work identity and unlock premium opportunities with secure wallet payments.
- Belief ladder:
  1. This platform is built for credible workers and their verification.
  2. Verified badges and wallet-based payments make the experience feel secure.
  3. Creating a profile is the fastest way to access premium work and verification benefits.
- Proof on hand: Verified badges, VIP status, wallet-based verification pricing, and the premium profile/CV workflow.

## Brand Personality

Trusted • Modern • Empowering. The voice should feel credible and contemporary, with a calm confidence that helps workers take the next step in their career.

## Anti-references

Do not look like a flashy gig marketplace, a casual social app, or a busy multi-colored consumer marketplace. Avoid loud green/yellow fintech clichés and overly aggressive social feed styling.

## Design Principles

- Treat trust as the primary brand signal: verified status and wallet flows should feel secure and easy to understand.
- Keep the visual system calm, premium, and modern, with strong contrast and clear hierarchy.
- Make conversion feel like progress: profile creation and verification should feel like an upgrade, not just a transaction.
- Emphasize clarity over decoration; every card, badge, and action should support confidence and ease.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Design for accessible contrast and clear hierarchy, with consideration for reduced motion and readable typography on mobile. Ensure text and interface elements remain usable for users with visual or cognitive needs.
